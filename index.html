SERVICE MENU

What We Offer Under Category 03

Treatments are selected based on site conditions and label-permitted uses for the product applied. If a requested pest/site is not label-permitted (or not within Category 03 scope), we will not apply.

Lawn & Turf Insect Management

Applications to turf areas for insects impacting lawns and outdoor use areas, per label directions.

  • Common turf pests (examples): grubs, chinch bugs, sod webworms, armyworms, mole crickets (as label-permitted).
  • Yard nuisance insects may be treatable when the product label allows the specific pest + turf site.

Ants, Fleas & Ticks (Yard / Turf)

Yard perimeter and turf treatments may be available when the selected product label specifically includes the target pest and the turf/ornamental site.

  • Ants in turf/landscape areas (not structural/indoor).
  • Fleas/ticks in lawns and outdoor use areas (label-permitted only).
  • We do not advertise or perform indoor/structural pest work under Category 03.

Ornamental & Shrub Treatments

Applications to ornamentals, shrubs, and landscape plantings for insects and related issues, per label directions.

  • Examples: aphids, mites, scale, bagworms, Japanese beetles (when label-permitted for the plant + pest).
  • Spot treatments and targeted approaches based on plant type and pressure.

Landscape Bed Applications

Label-permitted applications to landscape beds and non-crop vegetation areas on the property.

  • Insect management in beds/ornamental areas where the label permits use.
  • Options may include reduced-impact approaches when available.

Biting Insects / Outdoor Comfort

Outdoor nuisance/bite-pressure reduction is offered only when label-permitted for the target pest and application site, and when within KY Category 03 scope.

  • If the label/site does not allow it, we will not apply.
  • We do not provide public-health/vector control services.
Note: Examples above are not a guarantee of treatability. Final eligibility is determined by product label directions + site conditions + licensing scope.

FAQ

Common Questions

Straight answers with compliance in mind.
What can you do under Kentucky Category 03?
Category 03 is for lawn, turf, and ornamental/landscape vegetation applications. We focus on outdoor turf/ornamental sites only. We do not offer or advertise structural/indoor pest control services under Category 03.
Do you offer structural or indoor pest control?
No. This site advertises outdoor lawn/turf/ornamental applications only. If you need structural/indoor services, we can refer you to an appropriate provider.
How do you decide what to apply?
We evaluate the target area (turf, ornamentals, beds), the pest pressure, and the site conditions. We only apply products when the label permits the specific pest + application site, and the service falls within KY Category 03 scope.
Do you offer reduced-impact / eco-friendly options?
When available, yes. Eco-friendly options depend on the target pest/site and label-permitted uses. If an eco-friendly option is not appropriate or label-permitted, we will explain why and offer alternatives.
Can you treat mosquitoes?
We only provide outdoor biting-insect reduction when the selected product label specifically includes the target pest and the application site, and when the service fits within Category 03 scope. We do not provide public-health/vector control services. If your request isn’t eligible under label/scope, we will not apply and will recommend alternatives.
