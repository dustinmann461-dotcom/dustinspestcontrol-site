Common Questions

Straight answers with compliance in mind.
What can you do under Kentucky Category 03?

Category 03 generally relates to lawn, turf, and ornamental/landscape vegetation applications. We confirm each job based on the pesticide label (site + target pest) and the conditions on your property.

Do you offer structural or indoor pest control?

No. We do not advertise or provide structural pest control (Category 7A), indoor pest programs, termite work, or public health/vector control services.

How do you decide what to apply?

We select products and rates based on the target pest, the treatment site, and label directions. If the product label does not allow the target/site, we do not apply.

Do you offer reduced-impact / eco-friendly options?

Yes, when appropriate products are available and label-permitted for your target pest and site. Eco-friendly options are typically an upsell and may require a custom quote.
