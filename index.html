Common Questions

Answers written to stay consistent with Category 03 scope and label-directed requirements.

What can you do under Kentucky Category 03?

Category 03 generally covers pesticide applications to lawns, turf, ornamentals, and landscape/non-crop vegetation areas. The specific target pests, sites, rates, and application methods depend on the pesticide product label (the label is the law).

Do you offer structural/indoor pest control or termite work?

No. This business does not offer or advertise structural pest control (Category 7A), indoor pest programs, termite work, or public health vector control services.

How do you decide what to apply?

We confirm target treatment sites and the target pest situation, then select products and methods that are label-permitted for those sites and pests. Rates and methods follow label directions.

How often do treatments need to be done?

Timing depends on the target pest, weather, site conditions, and label directions for the products used. We’ll recommend a schedule based on what is appropriate for your property and within label guidance.

Do you have eco-friendly or reduced-impact options?

Yes—where suitable and label-permitted. Ask about reduced-impact approaches during scope review. Eco/reduced-impact upgrades are available (pricing shown above).
