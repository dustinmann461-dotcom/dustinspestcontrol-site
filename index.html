Common Questions

What’s the difference between barrier treatment and fogging?

Barrier treatment targets resting areas in the yard for longer control. Fogging provides faster immediate knockdown. Some customers choose both for stronger mosquito relief.

How long does mosquito treatment last?

Barrier treatments typically last around 3 to 4 weeks depending on weather, vegetation, and yard conditions.

Do you treat inside homes?

No. Dustin’s Pest Control is focused on outdoor turf, lawn, and ornamental treatment scope only.
